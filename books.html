Most-Watched Television Broadcast Of All Time

According to Wikipedia, the highest selling book of all time is "A Tale Of Two Cities" by Charles Dickens, published in 1859. It sold over 200 million copies as of 2025. This is only the highest selling book when excluding all books of a religious, ideological, philosophical or political nature; if those books are included, then The Bible drastically outsells it, with 5 billion copies sold.
