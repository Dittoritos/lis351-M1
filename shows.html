Most-Watched Television Broadcast of All Time (In The U.S.)

Since TV profits are based on watch numbers, and since I couldn't find a list that spanned all nations, I'll be using the highest-watched TV broadcast in the U.S. for this. According to Wikipedia, the most-watched television broadcast of all time in the U.S. is Super Bowl LIX, which took place in 2025. The game had 123 million viewers.
